## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Navigate to the Signup Page**
   - Click on "Sign up" link at the bottom of the login page

2. **Fill in Your Details**
   - **Full Name**: Enter your full name
   - **Email**: Enter a valid email address (this will be your username)
   - **Password**: Create a password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

3. **Submit**
   - Click "Create Account" button
   - You'll be automatically logged in and taken to the home page

### Logging In

1. **Enter Credentials**
   - Email: Your registered email address
   - Password: Your account password

2. **Toggle Password Visibility**
   - Click the eye icon to show/hide your password

3. **Sign In**
   - Click "Sign In" button
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### During the Quiz

#### Understanding the Interface

- **Timer**: Top right corner shows remaining time
- **Progress Bar**: Shows how many questions you've completed
- **Question Counter**: Displays current question number (e.g., "Question 5 of 10")
- **Answered Count**: Shows how many questions you've answered

#### Answering Questions

1. **Read the Question**
   - Each question has 4 multiple choice options

2. **Select Your Answer**
   - Click on any option to select it
   - Selected answers are highlighted in purple
   - A checkmark appears next to your selection

3. **Change Your Answer**
   - Click a different option to change your selection
   - You can change answers as many times as needed before submitting

#### Navigation

- **Next Button**: Move to the next question
- **Previous Button**: Go back to previous questions
- **Question Numbers**: Click any numbered button to jump to that question
  - Purple: Current question
  - Green: Answered questions
  - White: Unanswered questions

#### Submitting the Quiz

1. **Answer All Questions**
   - The Submit button is only enabled when all questions are answered
   - You can see the count: "10/10 answered"

2. **Time Management**
   - Keep an eye on the timer
   - If time runs out, the quiz will auto-submit
   - Try to answer all questions before time expires

3. **Submit**
   - On the last question, click "Submit Quiz"
   - You'll immediately see your results

#### Exit During Quiz

- Click "Exit Quiz" button in the top left
- Your progress will be lost
- You can retake the quiz anytime

## Managing Your Profile

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Click "Profile" in the navigation bar
2. Or click on your name in the top right corner (desktop)

### Profile Information

#### View Mode
- **Profile Picture**: Displays your initial (purple circle)
- **Name**: Your full name
- **Email**: Your registered email
- **Member Since**: Account creation date

#### Statistics Panel
- **Total Quizzes**: Unique quizzes you've completed
- **Average Score**: Your overall performance
- **Rank**: Your standing (e.g., "Top 10%")
- **Achievement Badge**: Current achievement status

### Editing Your Profile

1. **Click Edit Button**
   - Located in the top right of profile card

2. **Update Information**
   - **Full Name**: Change your name
   - **Email**: Update your email address

3. **Save Changes**
   - Click "Save Changes" to confirm
   - Or click "Cancel" to discard changes

### Important Notes
- Email must be unique
- Password cannot be changed in current version
- Changes are saved immediately to localStorage

## Tracking Your Progress

### Dashboard Statistics

Your home page displays key metrics:

#### Total Attempts
- Every quiz you take counts as an attempt
- Retaking a quiz adds another attempt
- Shows your overall quiz activity

#### Average Score
- Calculated from all your quiz attempts
- Updates after each quiz
- Helps track improvement over time

#### Best Score
- Your highest percentage achieved
- Motivates you to beat your record
- Shows your peak performance

#### Not Attempted
- Number of quizzes you haven't tried yet
- Encourages you to try all categories
- Goes down as you complete new quizzes

### Recent Activity

- Shows your last 3 quiz attempts
- Displays score, time taken, and score/total questions
- Quick access to see your latest performance

### Full Quiz History

#### Accessing History
1. Click "History" in navigation bar
2. Or click "View All" on Recent Activity section

#### History Page Features

**Summary Statistics**
- Total Quizzes taken
- Average Score across all attempts
- Best Score achieved

**Complete List**
- All quiz attempts listed chronologically
- Each entry shows:
  - Quiz title and category badge
  - Date completed
  - Time taken
  - Score and percentage
  - Color-coded percentage:
    - Green: 80%+ (Excellent)
    - Yellow: 60-79% (Good)
    - Red: Below 60% (Needs improvement)

## Tips for Success

### Before Taking a Quiz

1. **Choose the Right Time**
   - Pick a quiet time without distractions
   - Ensure you have enough time to complete it

2. **Check Your Internet**
   - While the app works offline, ensure page is loaded properly

3. **Review the Details**
   - Note the difficulty level
   - Check the time limit
   - Know the number of questions

### During the Quiz

1. **Read Carefully**
   - Read each question thoroughly
   - Don't rush through options

2. **Time Management**
   - Keep track of remaining time
   - Aim to spend equal time per question
   - Don't get stuck on one question

3. **Answer Strategy**
   - Answer questions you're confident about first
   - Use question navigation to skip difficult ones
   - Come back to skipped questions

4. **Stay Calm**
   - Don't panic if time is running low
   - Your first instinct is often correct
   - Trust your knowledge

### After the Quiz

1. **Review Your Score**
   - Note which areas you did well in
   - Identify topics to study more

2. **Track Improvement**
   - Compare scores with previous attempts
   - Watch your average score increase

3. **Retake Strategically**
   - Retake quizzes where you scored low
   - Try to beat your best score

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Can't log in
- **Solution**: 
  - Double-check your email and password
  - Email is case-insensitive but password is case-sensitive
  - Ensure you registered with this email

**Problem**: Forgot password
- **Solution**: 
  - Currently no password recovery feature
  - Create a new account if needed

### Quiz Issues

**Problem**: Quiz timer not working
- **Solution**: 
  - Refresh the page
  - Ensure JavaScript is enabled

**Problem**: Can't submit quiz
- **Solution**: 
  - Ensure all questions are answered
  - Check the "answered" count at the top

**Problem**: Lost progress during quiz
- **Solution**: 
  - If you exit or refresh, progress is lost
  - Complete quiz in one session

### Data Issues

**Problem**: Lost all my data
- **Solution**: 
  - Check if browser data/localStorage was cleared
  - Data is stored locally per browser
  - Can't be recovered if cleared

**Problem**: Stats not updating
- **Solution**: 
  - Refresh the page
  - Check browser console for errors
  - Try logging out and back in

### Display Issues

**Problem**: Layout looks broken
- **Solution**: 
  - Ensure you're using a modern browser
  - Clear browser cache
  - Try a different browser

**Problem**: Mobile menu not working
- **Solution**: 
  - Tap the hamburger icon (three lines)
  - Refresh the page if needed

## Data Management

### Backing Up Your Data

Your data is stored in localStorage. To backup:

1. Open browser console (F12)
2. Go to Application/Storage tab
3. Find localStorage entries starting with "quiz_app_"
4. Copy the values to a text file

### Clearing Your Data

To start fresh:

1. **Logout**: Click logout button
2. **Clear Browser Data**: 
   - Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
   - Select "Cookies and site data"
   - Or just clear for this specific site

### Privacy

- All data stays on your device
- No data is sent to any server
- No tracking or cookies
- Safe to use in private/incognito mode (but data won't persist)
